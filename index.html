How to Play Tango

  • Balance: Every row and column must contain exactly 3 suns (☀️) and 3 moons (🌙).
  • No Triplet: No three consecutive identical symbols are allowed in any row or column.
  • Adjacency Markers:
    `=` means the two touching cells must be the same.
    `×` means they must be opposite.
  • Uniqueness: No two rows can be identical; no two columns can be identical.

Use logic (no guessing!) to fill the board.
